Humanities Teacher & Project Designer High Tech High, 2016–2019

Postdoctoral Scholar University of Düsseldorf, 2013–2014

Following work on my dissertation, I began research on the relationship between word frequency and morphological behavior.

Research & Teaching Assistant Stanford University, 2007–2012
